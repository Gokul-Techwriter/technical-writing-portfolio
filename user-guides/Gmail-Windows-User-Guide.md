## 1. Overview

Gmail is a free email service from Google that allows you to send and receive emails on computers and mobile devices. This guide explains to users how to use Gmail and its features on Windows.


## 2.Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure the following requirements are met:  

- Stable internet connection
- Valid Google account

## 3. Key Features

- 15 GB of shared storage (Google account).  
- Strong protection is provided against spam and phishing.    
- Email searches are quick and easy.    
- Files can be attached using Google Drive.    
- Access is available on the web, desktop, and mobile devices.    
- It integrates with Google services like Calendar and Google Meet.

---

## 4. Procedure: Using Gmail

**Note:** Gmail is a web-based service that cannot be installed as a standalone application on Windows.

### 4.1 Launch the Gmail Website

1. Open a supported web browser on your desktop (Chrome, Firefox, Microsoft Edge, etc).

2. Go to the official website of Gmail.

**Result:** The official Gmail website opened without any error.

### 4.2 Log In

1. Enter your email address and password.
2. Click **Next.**

**Result:** logged into Gmail without any errors.

### 4.3 Sending an Email

1. Click **Compose** on the top left side of the screen.

2. Enter the recipient's email address in the **To** section.

3. Enter the subject of the email.

4. Type the content you wish to send > click **Send**.

**Result:** Email has been sent without errors.

## 6. Troubleshooting

**Problem 1: Email Sending Failed**  
**Causes:** Unstable internet connection or an error in the given details.

**Solution:**  

1. Click **Start** > **Settings** > **Network & Internet**  
2. Ensure the system has a stable internet connection.  
3. Refresh the browser.  
4. Verify that the entered details are correct.  
5. Resend the email.  

**Result:** Email sent without any errors.

**Problem 2: Gmail Account Sign-In Failed**  
**Causes:** Wrong password or email address.    

**Solution:**  

1. Refresh the browser.    
2. Enter the correct email address.    
3. Enter the correct password > click **Next**.

**Result:** logged into Gmail without errors.

## 7. FAQ

**Q1. Can we send any files through Gmail?**

**A:** Yes, you can send any attachment up to 25 MB in a single email. For larger files, you can share a link via Google Drive.

**Q2. Is Gmail secure?**

**A:** Yes, Gmail utilizes encryption (such as TLS) to safeguard emails, automatically filters out spam and malware, and offers robust security features, including two-factor authentication and alerts for suspicious activity. However, user safety also depends on using strong passwords and avoiding phishing emails.
