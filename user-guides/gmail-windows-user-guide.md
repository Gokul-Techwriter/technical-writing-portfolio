## Purpose

## Intended Audience

This guide is intended for:

- Users with basic computer skills

## 1. Overview

Gmail is a free email service from Google that allows you to send and receive emails on Windows via a web browser. This guide explains to users how to use Gmail and its features on Windows.

## 2.Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure the following requirements are met:  

- Stable internet connection
- Windows 10 or later
- Supported Browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, etc.)
- Valid Google account

## 3. Key Features

- 15 GB of shared storage (Google account).  
- Built-in spam and phishing protection.    
- Email searches are quick and easy.    
- Share large files as Google Drive links directly within emails.
- Access is available on the web, and mobile devices.    
- It integrates with Google services like Calendar and Google Meet.

## 4. Procedure: Using Gmail

**Note:** Gmail is a web-based service that cannot be installed as a standalone application on Windows.

### 4.1 Launch the Gmail Website

1. Open a supported web browser on your desktop (Chrome, Firefox, Microsoft Edge, etc).

2. Go to the official website of Gmail:  https://mail.google.com

**Result:** The official Gmail website launched successfully.

### 4.2 Log In

1. Enter your email address and password

2. Click **Next**

3. If two-factor authentication is enabled, enter the verification code sent to your registered device or authentication app.

**Result:** Logged into Gmail without errors.

### 4.3 Sending an Email

1. Click **Compose** on the left sidebar

2. Enter the recipient's email address in the **To** section

3. Enter the subject of the email

4. Type the content you wish to send > click **Send**

**Result:** Email has been sent without errors.

## 6. Troubleshooting

**Problem 1:** Email Sending Failed  
**Causes:** Unstable internet connection or an error in the given details.

**Solution:**  

1. Click **Start** > **Settings** > **Network & Internet**(Windows 10) or **Settings** > **Network & Internet** > **Advanced network settings**(Windows 11)
2. Ensure the system has a stable internet connection
3. Verify the recipient's email address is correct
4. Refresh the browser
5. Verify that the entered details are correct  
6. Resend the email

**Result:** Email has been sent successfully.

**Problem 2:** Gmail Account Sign-In Failed  
**Causes:** Wrong password or email address.    

**Solution:**  

1. Refresh the browser
2. Enter the correct email address
3. Enter the correct password > click **Next**
4. If you have forgotten your password, click **Forgot password?** on the sign-in page and follow the account recovery steps

**Result:** Logged into Gmail without errors.

## 7. FAQ

**Q1. What types of files can be sent through Gmail, and what is the size limit?**

**A:** You can send most file types up to 25 MB. Note that Gmail blocks executable file types such as .exe and .apk for security reasons. For files larger than 25 MB, share a Google Drive link instead.

**Q2. Is Gmail secure?**

**A:** Yes, Gmail utilizes encryption (such as TLS) to safeguard emails, automatically filters out spam and malware, and offers robust security features, including two-factor authentication and alerts for suspicious activity. However, user safety also depends on using strong passwords and avoiding phishing emails.
