# Enpass App for Windows and Android

---

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This guide explains how to install, verify, and uninstall the Enpass app on both Windows and Android.

### 1.2 Scope

This guide covers:

- System requirements  
- Installation procedure
- Verification  
- Uninstalling procedure

### 1.3 Intended Audience

This guide is intended for: 

- End users
- System administrators
- IT Support teams

---

## 2. Overview 

Enpass is a secure, offline-first password and passkey manager. It stores your encrypted data locally on your device or in your chosen cloud storage. Enpass does not store user data on its own servers, giving you full control over your data and privacy.

## 3. System Requirements

Before you begin, ensure the following requirements are met:

### 3.1 Software Requirements

On Windows:
- A stable internet connection 
- Windows 10 or later

On Android: 
- A stable internet connection
- Android 9 or later

### 3.2 Hardware Requirements

On Windows: 
- Minimum 4 GB of RAM
- Minimum 300 MB of free disk space
- Modern Intel/AMD CPU

On Android: 
- Minimum 2 GB of RAM
- Minimum 50 MB of storage space

## 4. Pre-Installation Checklist

Before you install the Enpass app, ensure that you meet the following requirements:

- Stable internet connection.  
- Administrative access.  
- Basic system/device specifications.

### 5.2 Install Enpass app

Follow the steps to install the app.

1. Open the **Downloads folder.**

   

   

   

   

   

   

2. Double-click the **EnpassApp.exe file**.

   

   

   

   

 


   

3. Agree to the **Terms and Conditions;** click **Install**. 

   

   

   

   

   

   

   

   

4. Wait for the app to **install**.

   

**Result:** Enpass app installed on Windows.

On Android:

1. Open the **Google Play Store**.

## 7. Uninstall the Enpass app

Follow the steps to uninstall the app.

- On Windows:

1. Click **Start** > **Settings** > **Apps** > **Apps & features**(may vary according to the version)  
     
     
     
2. Find the Enpass app > click the **Enpass app icon.**  
     
     
     
     
     
     
3. Click **Uninstall** and wait for the app to get uninstalled.  
     
     
     
     
     
**Result:** Enpass app uninstalled on Windows.

- On Android

1. Long-press on the **Enpass app icon** > click **Uninstall**.

2. Tap **Uninstall** again and wait for the app to be uninstalled.

**Result:** Enpass app uninstalled on Android.

## 8. Troubleshooting

**Problem 1:** Website not loading on Windows  
**Cause:** Unstable internet connection.  

**Solution:**

1. Click **Start** > **Settings** > **Network & Internet**  
2. Ensure proper internet connection.  
3. Reopen the **Enpass website**.

**Problem 2:** The app is not opening
**Cause:** Basic requirements are not met.  

**Solution:**

- On Windows  
1. Click **Start** > **Settings** > **System** > **About**  
2. Make sure the basic requirements are met.  
3. Relaunch the **Enpass app**.

- On Android  
1. Open **Settings** on your device > **About Phone**  
2. Make sure the basic requirements are met.  
3. Reopen the **Enpass app**.
