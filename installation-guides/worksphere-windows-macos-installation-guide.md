## 2. Overview

WorkSphere Desktop is a project management and team collaboration app that helps teams organize tasks, track progress, manage workflows, and sync in real time on Windows and macOS.

## 3. System Requirements

Before you begin, ensure the following requirements are met:

### 3.1 Software Requirements

- Windows(Windows 10 or later)

- macOS(macOS 12 Monterey or later)

### 3.2 Hardware Requirements

On Windows:
- Minimum 4 GB of RAM  
- Intel Core i3 (8th generation or later) or AMD Ryzen 3 or equivalent
- Minimum clock speed: 2.0 GHz dual-core or better
- Minimum 500 MB of free disk space

On macOS:
- Minimum 4 GB of RAM
- Apple Silicon (M1 or later) or Intel Core i5 or equivalent
- Minimum: Dual-core 2.0 GHz or better
- Minimum 500 MB of free disk space

## 4. Pre-Installation Checklist

Before you install the WorkSphere Desktop app, ensure that you meet the following requirements:

- A proper internet connection.  
- Meet the system requirements listed above.

## 8. Troubleshooting

**Problem 1:** Website loading fails
**Cause:** No stable internet connection

**Solution:**

- On Windows:  
1. Open **Start Menu** > **Settings** > **Network & Internet**  
2. Check that the system has a stable internet connection
3. Reopen the **WorkSphere Desktop website**

- On macOS:  
1. Click the **Apple menu** > choose **System Settings**  
2. Select **Network**.  
3. Check that the system has a stable internet connection.  
4. Reopen the **WorkSphere Desktop website**.

**Problem 2:** Can't uninstall the app
**Cause:** App running in the background

**Solution:**

1. Do the following:  
- On Windows: check the **Task bar**.  
- On macOS: check the **Dock**.  
2. Close the **WorkSphere Desktop app** that is running in the background.  
3. Restart the uninstalling procedure.
